1. v-show

The element with v-show will always be rendered and remain in the DOM, it only toggles the display CSS property of the element

2. v-if VS v-show

  1. The block with v-if won't be rendered until the conditions becomes true for first time,

    and this block will be properly destroyed and re-created during toggles

  2. The element with v-show always rendered regardless of initial condition, with CSS-based toggling.
  3. Prefer v-show if you need to toggle something very often
    Prefer v-if if the condition is unlikely to change at runtime.
